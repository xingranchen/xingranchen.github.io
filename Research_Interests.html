Interests

My research interests focus on networked systems, with particular emphasis on: (i) security, (ii) freshness, and (iii) decentralized learning.

My work draws on techniques from the following areas:

  • Theory: stochastic processes, information theory, graph theory
  • Learning: multi-agent reinforcement learning, distributed optimization
