Research Fields

  • Research Interests: My research interests are in the modeling, analysis, and decision making of distributed networked systems (e.g., the Internet of Things, communication networks, Blockchain), with emphasis on information freshness, resource allocation, economics, and security.

  • Methodologies: Information and Coding Theory, Learning Theory, Optimization, Game Theory, and Control Theory.
