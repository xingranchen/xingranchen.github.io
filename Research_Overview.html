Research Overview

Research Interests

My research focuses on learning, information processing and transfer, and security in decentralized networked systems, particularly in:

  • decentralized learning and large language models;
  • security, privacy, and robustness;
  • networking and multi-agent systems.
My work draws on the following fundamental tools:
  • Theory: information theory, stochastic processes, and graph theory.
  • Learning: foundation models and alignment, decentralized optimization, and reinforcement learning.
