Research Overview

Research Interests

My research interests focus on the fundamental limits and theoretical guarantees for decentralized systems, with particular emphasis on:

  • networking and multi-agent systems;
  • cybersecurity, privacy, and robustness;
  • distributed information processing.

My work draws on techniques from the following areas:

  • Theory: stochastic processes, information theory, graph theory
  • Learning: reinforcement learning, distributed optimization
