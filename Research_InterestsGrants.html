Research Interests

  • My research interests lie at the intersection of information theory, machine learning, and network science.

  • My recent research focuses on the performance analysis, learning, and control of radio access networks in the context of the Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT), with an emphasis on freshness-oriented scenarios.
